epik-picker 业务选择器

概述
  • epik-picker用于选择业务相关的数据
  • 有以下几种形式的选择器
    • epik-customer-picker: 选择客户
    • epik-industry-picker: 选择行业
    • epik-user-picker: 选择员工
实例
epik-customer-picker参数
  • ng-modal,要绑定的字段，数组
  • required,是否必填
代码 
<form name="form1" class="form-horizontal" novalidate>
  <div class="form-group">
    <label class="control-label col-md-2">选择客户</label>
    <div class="col-md-4">
      <epik-customer-picker name="customer" ng-model="vm.customers" required></epik-customer-picker>
      <div class="text-danger" ng-messages="form1.customer.$error || form1.customer.$error">
        <div ng-messages-include="/components/tpls/errorMessages.html"></div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</form>

$scope.vm = {
  customers : ['85B83978-E2BD-446F-94C4-B2DC7C8AEAB8', '5479CD69-D7CE-46B1-8EFB-A47E4F1E8690']
}
