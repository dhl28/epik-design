upload&download 上传下载

概述
  • 文件上传使用angularFileUpload3方控件，https://github.com/nervgh/angular-file-upload
  • 对angularFileUpload作了2次封装，提供epik-uploader-modal, epik-downloader, epik-downloader-modal指令
  • 指令
    • epik-uploader-modal：文件上传弹出框(弹出)
        用法
      • <epik-uploader-modal file-list="fileList" enforce-length="4" enforce-max-file-size="4*1024*1024" enforce-type="'(png|jpeg|jpg|gif)'"></epik-uploader-modal>
      • file-list: 上传文件成功后返回的数组, format: [{documentId: "026E672E-EA77-4FC2-9EBC-A81C0A6EA8E8" filename: "Hydrangeas.jpg"},...]
      • enforce-length: 上传文件个数
      • enforce-max-file-size: 单个文件大小限制
      • enforce-type: 文件类型限制
    • epik-downloader: 文件下载列表(嵌入)
        用法
      • <epik-downloader file-list="fileList"></epik-downloader>
      • file-list: 下载文件对应数组, format: [{documentId: "026E672E-EA77-4FC2-9EBC-A81C0A6EA8E8" filename: "Hydrangeas.jpg"},...]
    • epik-downloader-modal: 文件下载列表框(弹出)
      • <epik-downloader-modal file-list="fileList"></epik-downloader-modal>
      • file-list: 下载文件对应数组, format: [{documentId: "026E672E-EA77-4FC2-9EBC-A81C0A6EA8E8" filename: "Hydrangeas.jpg"},...]
实例
代码 
<form name="form1" class="form-horizontal" novalidate>

  </div>
  <div class="col-md-6">
    <epik-downloader file-list="fileList"></epik-downloader>
  </div>
</div>
</form>
